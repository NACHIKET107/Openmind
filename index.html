About This Blog

OPENMIND is my personal space on the internet — a place to think out loud about life, India, the world, faith, and everything in between.

I believe in writing honestly, even when it's uncomfortable. In staying curious about things that challenge my assumptions. And in the idea that ordinary people living ordinary lives have extraordinary things to say, if they take the time to say them.

This is my attempt to do that. I'm glad you're reading it.
